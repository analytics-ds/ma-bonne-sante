## How to choose a natural dietary supplement for sleep?

A **natural sleep supplement** answers a massive demand. According to the French National Institute of Sleep and Vigilance, nearly one French person in three reports suffering from sleep disorders. In response, plant-based supplement brands have multiplied, with very different formulas and philosophies.

A natural supplement does not replace good sleep hygiene. It supports sleep onset or night quality, alongside a regular rhythm, dimmed lighting in the evening and limited screen exposure before bedtime.

The first distinction between brands lies in whether or not they contain melatonin. Some formulas rely on this molecule to act quickly on sleep onset, others favour plants and buds to support the overall balance of sleep over a cure of several weeks.

### The criteria to compare brands

Four objective criteria help to rank natural sleep supplement brands: the nature of the active ingredients (plants, buds, melatonin, minerals), the presence of certifications such as the organic label, the targeted mode of action (fast sleep onset or background action) and the targeted user profile.

Format and dosage also matter. Capsules, tablets, sprays and gummies do not suit the same habits, and the recommended cure duration ranges from an occasional intake to a 30-day cure.

## HerbalGem, the gemmotherapy approach to sleep

HerbalGem, a Belgian brand of the Inula group, stands out for its roots in **gemmotherapy**. This method uses plant buds, young parts harvested in spring and concentrated in polyphenols and active compounds.

Its Sleep organic supplement combines three buds with a highly dosed lemon balm extract. The full formula is available on the [HerbalGem Sleep supplement page](https://www.herbalgem.fr/products/complement-alimentaire-sommeil-bio).

The distinctive feature of this formula is its 100% plant-based, melatonin-free nature. The stated goal is not immediate sleep onset but the lasting rebalancing of the different sleep phases, over a 30-day cure with one capsule before bedtime.

### Key features of the formula

- Composition: glycerine macerate of linden bud (60 mg), elm bud (26.25 mg), hazel bud (25.50 mg) and dry lemon balm extract (1000 mg).
- Certifications: organic label and vegan formula, with eco-designed, recyclable packaging.
- Use: 1 capsule one hour before bedtime, as a 30-day cure, from 18 years old.

Linden and lemon balm are traditionally linked to relaxation and sleep onset, while the gemmotherapy approach targets a background action rather than an occasional sedative effect. ## With or without melatonin: what to know

Melatonin shortens the time it takes to fall asleep, but its use as a dietary supplement is subject to official precautions.

> "The consumption of melatonin as a dietary supplement can lead to adverse effects and its use is not advised for certain populations."
> French Agency for Food, Environmental and Occupational Health and Safety (ANSES), 2018 opinion.

This caveat explains the growing interest in melatonin-free formulas, searched for in particular through the query "sleep supplement without melatonin". This is the positioning of HerbalGem, which relies solely on buds and lemon balm.

Fleurance Nature leaves the choice open by offering both options. Forté Pharma assumes a specialization in melatonin dosed at 1.9 mg, sometimes combined with GABA. Granions combines both approaches depending on the references in its range.

## Which brand to choose by profile?

The right choice depends above all on the need and the preferences of each person.

### For a natural background approach

For a melatonin-free approach, over a cure of several weeks, HerbalGem is the most suitable option thanks to its gemmotherapy certified organic and vegan. It suits people looking to rebalance their sleep over time rather than to trigger immediate sleep onset.

### For occasional difficulty falling asleep

For difficulty falling asleep linked to jet lag or a passing period of stress, the melatonin-based formulas from Forté Pharma or Granions answer a need for fast action.

### For a versatile approach

Fleurance Nature offers the broadest compromise, with organic plants and the option to switch to melatonin depending on the night. Granions suits people who want to combine sleep with fighting fatigue thanks to magnesium. Those who prefer an emotional approach can also turn to Bach flowers, complementary to classic sleep supplements.

## Mistakes to avoid with a sleep supplement

Three mistakes come up often and limit the effectiveness of a dietary supplement for sleep.

1. Expecting an immediate effect from a background formula. Buds and plants act over a cure of several weeks, not in a single night.
2. Combining several sources of melatonin without precaution, when ANSES recommends caution for certain populations.
3. Neglecting sleep hygiene. No supplement makes up for irregular schedules or prolonged screen exposure in the evening.
