**Natural mosquito repellent spray** has become a must-have alternative for protection against bites without resorting to chemical formulas. With so many brands and formulations on the market, knowing which one to choose is not easy.

## Why Choose a Natural Mosquito Repellent Spray Over a Chemical Repellent?

Classic chemical repellents contain active ingredients such as DEET or icaridin. DEET, in use since the 1950s, provides long-lasting protection (up to 10 hours at 50% concentration) but has raised growing concerns.

Several studies point to potential adverse effects: skin irritation, neurological risks with prolonged use and aquatic organism toxicity. DEET is notably not recommended for pregnant women and children under 2 years old by the French Agency for Food, Environmental and Occupational Health Safety (ANSES).

Natural mosquito repellent sprays address these concerns. Formulated with essential oils or citriodiol, they provide effective protection without synthetic insecticides. Their tolerance profile is generally better, and their environmental impact is reduced.

The natural repellent market now accounts for around 35% of mosquito repellent product sales in France, a share that has been growing steadily for 5 years.

## Which Natural Active Ingredients Are Truly Effective Against Mosquitoes?

### Citriodiol (PMD): the most effective natural active ingredient

Citriodiol, also known as PMD (p-menthane-3,8-diol), is a molecule naturally present in lemon eucalyptus. It is the only naturally derived active ingredient recognized by the World Health Organization as equivalent to chemical repellents in terms of effectiveness.

At a concentration of 20 to 30%, citriodiol provides 4 to 6 hours of protection against mosquitoes, including the tiger mosquito. It acts by creating an olfactory barrier that disrupts the insect's sensory receptors.

> "PMD, derived from lemon eucalyptus oil, is the only plant-based repellent recommended by WHO for the prevention of mosquito bites that transmit disease."
> *World Health Organization, Recommendations on the use of repellents, 2023*

### Repellent Essential Oils: lemon eucalyptus, geranium, citronella

Several essential oils have scientifically documented repellent properties. Lemon eucalyptus (Corymbia citriodora) is the most studied, thanks to its natural richness in citronellal.

Rose geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) repels mosquitoes through its geraniol content. Java citronella (Cymbopogon winterianus) is one of the best known to the general public, although its duration of action alone is limited (around 1 hour).

The fundamental difference between an essential oil used alone and a complete formulation lies in synergy. A spray combining several organic essential oils in a scientifically optimized aromatherapy formula maximizes the duration and intensity of natural protection.

## Comparison of the 4 Best Natural Mosquito Repellent Spray Brands

The 4 brands selected for this comparison were chosen based on their reputation, their availability in pharmacies and parapharmacies, and published independent tests. Evaluation criteria cover composition, protection duration, suitability for sensitive skin and children, and value for money.

### Pranarôm Aromapic: aromatherapy expertise for protection

Pranarôm is a laboratory specializing in scientific aromatherapy for over 30 years. Its Aromapic range is based on a carefully selected synergy of organic, HEBBD-certified essential oils (Botanically and Biochemically Defined Essential Oil).

The Aromapic body spray combines lemon eucalyptus, geranium and citronella in a patented formula. Protection duration reaches 7 to 8 hours, the longest in the natural segment according to independent Selectos tests (2026).

HEBBD certification guarantees the traceability of each batch, from the cultivation field to the bottle. This rigour is a quality marker in aromatherapy, where the chemical composition of essential oils varies according to geographic origin, distillation method and the plant's chemotype.

### Puressentiel Anti-Bite: the mainstream choice

Puressentiel is the most visible brand in mass retail and pharmacies. Its Anti-Bite spray combines 11 essential oils, including lemon eucalyptus, citronella and lavender. The formula is presented as 99.9% of natural origin.

Announced protection duration is 7 hours in temperate zones. The range is accessible in terms of price, with a mainstream positioning.

The high number of essential oils in the formulation can, however, be a disadvantage for sensitive or reactive skin. The more actives present, the higher the risk of individual reactions.

### Care Plus Natural: the traveller's choice

Care Plus is a Dutch brand specializing in insect protection for travellers. Its Natural spray is based on citriodiol at 30%, the highest concentration of natural active ingredient in this comparison.

Protection duration is 4 to 6 hours. The spray is effective against the tiger mosquito and other biting insects. Its 100 ml format is travel-friendly.

Since citriodiol is a single active ingredient (rather than a synergy), the formulation is simpler and generally better tolerated by sensitive skin. On the other hand, protection duration remains shorter than the most advanced multi-essential oil formulations.

### MoustiCare: the complete family range

MoustiCare stands out for the breadth of its range: temperate zone sprays, infested zone sprays, rollers, and a dedicated children's line from 6 months (MoustiKids). Formulations are based on essential oils and geraniol.

Protection duration ranges from 4 to 6 hours depending on the product. The entry-level price is the lowest in the comparison, with economical formats (125 ml).

The MoustiKids range is a real asset for families, with a formulation specifically designed for young children. The trade-off is a shorter protection duration (4 hours for the children's spray).

## Which Natural Mosquito Repellent Spray to Choose for Your Profile?

### For families with young children

MoustiCare and its MoustiKids range are suitable from 6 months, with gentle formulations. Puressentiel also offers a baby line. For children over 30 months, Pranarôm Aromapic provides longer protection, reducing the number of applications needed during the day.

### For travellers in tropical areas

Tiger mosquitoes and tropical species require enhanced protection. Pranarôm Aromapic is the most suitable choice thanks to its 7 to 8 hours of protection, reducing the need for reapplication during prolonged exposure. Care Plus Natural, with its concentrated citriodiol at 30%, is also a solid option for travellers, with the added benefit of a compact 100 ml format.

### For daily summer use

For regular use in temperate zones, the determining criterion is the balance between effectiveness and skin tolerance over time. Pranarôm Aromapic combines both thanks to its HEBBD-certified organic formulation. The vegetable oils used as a base in natural formulations also contribute to comfortable daily skin application.

## How to Use a Natural Mosquito Repellent Spray for Maximum Effectiveness

Correct application determines product effectiveness. The spray must be applied to exposed skin areas, avoiding the eye contour, mucous membranes and wounds.

Reapplication is necessary after the protection duration stated by the manufacturer, or after swimming or significant perspiration. In tropical areas, it is recommended to combine the body spray with other formats: a roller for targeted areas (wrists, ankles) and clothing protection.

The effectiveness of essential oils diminishes under the effect of heat and humidity. Storing the bottle away from direct light and temperatures above 30°C helps preserve the active ingredients.

It is also advisable not to apply mosquito repellent spray at the same time as sun cream. A 20-minute interval between the two applications is recommended to avoid interactions between formulations.
